StreamerUniBot Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 19, 2026

1. Introduction

StreamerUniBot is a content automation tool that helps creators manage, process, and publish streamer clips across social media platforms. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use StreamerUniBot.

2. Information We Access

StreamerUniBot may access information provided through connected third-party platforms, including TikTok, YouTube, and Twitch APIs. This may include:

  • Basic account information needed to authenticate your account.
  • Authorization tokens required to perform approved actions.
  • Video and clip information needed for publishing features.

3. How We Use Information

Information accessed by StreamerUniBot is used only to provide its features, including:

  • Finding and managing streamer clips.
  • Uploading approved content to connected platforms.
  • Maintaining account connections and API functionality.

4. Data Storage

StreamerUniBot does not sell, rent, or share user information with third parties. Authentication data is used only for providing the requested service.

5. Third-Party Platforms

StreamerUniBot connects with third-party services including TikTok, YouTube, and Twitch. These platforms have their own privacy policies and terms of service. Users should review those policies separately.

6. User Control

Users can disconnect StreamerUniBot from connected platforms at any time by removing the application's permissions through their account settings on those platforms.

7. Security

Reasonable measures are taken to protect information used by the application. However, no online service can guarantee complete security.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Continued use of StreamerUniBot after changes means you accept the updated policy.

9. Contact

For questions about this Privacy Policy, contact StreamerUniBot Support.
